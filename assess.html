Self-Assessment

This is my self evaluation of four categories throughout ESP 252: Communication, Deadlines Attendance, and Participation.

  1. Communication: 1.5

    I did not keep in touch with the professor as often as I should have. I only sent three emails over the course of the class, one of which was the first assignment given. I didn't go to office hours despite the fact that I needed guidance with multiple projects. The only saving grace is that I wrote most of the emails in a professional tone and kept them short.

  2. Deadlines: 0.9

    I was extremely bad at meeting dealines. I missed multiple assignments with both set and ambiguous deadlines, and emailed only to have deadlines rescheduled for later dates. Even with later deadlines, I still mangaged to wait until the last possible moment to get things done.

  3. Attendance: 3.5

    I only missed two classes over the course of 252. One was because of the fall career fair, and the other was because I was sick. I didn't communicate mmy sickness in a timely manner, which also plays into my low score for communication.

  4. Participation: 3.3

    I took every assignment to heart. While I didn't complete them when I should have (if at all), the ones I did finish left me with more questions. I struggled with 252. I think the struggling is what made me sign up for 351. I want to get a better understanding of the material, and I want to properly devote time to learning it.
